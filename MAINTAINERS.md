## Maintained Scope

VulnLab APK is maintained as an intentionally vulnerable Android training app.
The maintained scope is:

- OWASP Mobile Top 10-aligned vulnerable components
- Android-specific attack surface demonstrations
- build instructions for Android SDK tooling
- safe lab setup documentation
- exploit reference commands for authorized local labs
- screenshots and walkthrough documentation

Out of scope:

- deployment on production devices
- bypassing third-party apps or real services
- credential theft against real users
- malware behavior or persistence outside the lab app

## Maintenance Commitments

- Keep all vulnerabilities intentional and documented.
- Keep the package name and lab scope stable.
- Review reports that improve accuracy, safety, or reproducibility.
- Avoid adding live malware, real credentials, or third-party target material.
- Prefer lightweight tests that verify the manifest, docs, and source coverage.
